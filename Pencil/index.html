Objective

I've loved data and design for as long as I can remember. Attending Barnard College, Columbia University I set out to become an environmental engineer. After I realized that lab work didn't quite scratch my creative itch, I dove head first into the film industry. After a short stint working on film sets, I said goodbye to the Big Apple and headed back to Nashville, my hometown. There, I put my photography skills to use in a foreign industry--marketing-- teaching myself the ropes and learning on the job, I worked for various small businesses on a contract basis for three years. Until finally, I stumbled upon an industry that married my two passions perfectly: software development.
